================================================================================
                    KNOWTING - PROMOTIONAL VIDEO SCRIPT
================================================================================

Duration: Approximately 3-4 minutes
Target Audience: Students, educators, and lifelong learners

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENE 1: HOOK (0:00 - 0:15)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Visual: Person struggling with scattered notes and textbooks]

NARRATOR:
"Tired of ineffective study methods? Struggling to track your learning progress?
There's a better way to master any subject."

[Visual: Transition to clean Knowting interface]

"Introducing Knowting — your personal learning companion."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENE 2: WHAT IS KNOWTING? (0:15 - 0:45)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Visual: Overview of the Knowting home page]

NARRATOR:
"Knowting is a powerful web platform designed for creating, sharing, and taking
educational tests. Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a teacher
building assessments, or a professional staying sharp — Knowting adapts to
your needs."

[Visual: Show test creation interface]

"Create custom tests with multiple-choice questions, add explanations for each
answer, and organize everything by topics. Learning has never been this
structured."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENE 3: KEY FEATURES (0:45 - 2:00)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Visual: Creating a test]

NARRATOR:
"Let's explore what makes Knowting special."

--- Feature 1: Easy Test Creation ---

"Creating a test is simple. Add a title, write your questions, define the
correct answers, and include helpful explanations. Your students will
understand not just WHAT the answer is, but WHY."

[Visual: Adding study materials - videos, PDFs, images]

--- Feature 2: Rich Study Materials ---

"Enhance your tests with study materials. Upload videos, PDFs, and images
directly to your tests. Everything your learners need, all in one place."

[Visual: Collaboration interface - inviting users]

--- Feature 3: Seamless Collaboration ---

"Learning is better together. Invite collaborators with different roles:
- Students can take tests
- Guests can view content and practice
- Reviewers can help edit questions
- Administrators can manage everything

Share knowledge, build together, grow together."

[Visual: Statistics and progress charts]

--- Feature 4: Track Your Progress ---

"See your improvement over time. Knowting tracks every answer, calculates
your statistics by topic, and shows you exactly where to focus your efforts.
No more guessing — just data-driven learning."

[Visual: Courses/Programs overview]

--- Feature 5: Organize with Courses ---

"Group related tests into courses. Perfect for structured learning paths,
exam preparation, or complete curricula. Your content, beautifully organized."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENE 4: USER PLANS (2:00 - 2:30)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Visual: Show the three plan columns from the home page]

NARRATOR:
"Knowting offers flexible access for everyone."

[Visual: Highlight Visitor section]

"As a visitor, explore public tests and discover what's possible."

[Visual: Highlight Knower section]

"Register for free as a Knower. Create unlimited tests, upload materials,
track your progress, and collaborate with others."

[Visual: Highlight Knowter section]

"Become a Knowter to unlock premium features like creating courses,
advanced visibility controls, and exclusive content access."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENE 5: HOW TO GET STARTED (2:30 - 3:00)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Visual: Google Sign-In button]

NARRATOR:
"Getting started takes seconds. Sign in with your Google account — no lengthy
registration forms, no passwords to remember."

[Visual: Quick walkthrough - creating first test]

"Create your first test in minutes. Add questions, set visibility options,
and share with your study group instantly."

[Visual: Taking a test on mobile/tablet]

"Access your tests anywhere, anytime. Knowting works on any device with a
web browser."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENE 6: CALL TO ACTION (3:20 - 3:45)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Visual: Knowting logo with sign-up prompt]

NARRATOR:
"Ready to transform how you learn?"

[Visual: Happy students/professionals using the platform]

"Join thousands of learners who've discovered the power of structured,
trackable, collaborative learning."

[Visual: Website URL and sign-up button]

"Visit Knowting today. Sign up for free. Start learning smarter."

[Visual: Knowting logo animation]

"Knowting — Know more. Test better. Learn smarter."

================================================================================
                           END OF SCRIPT

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRODUCTION NOTES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

MUSIC SUGGESTIONS:
- Upbeat, inspiring background music
- Softer during feature explanations
- Build to crescendo for call to action

VISUAL STYLE:
- Clean, modern interface shots
- Smooth transitions between scenes
- Highlight UI elements with subtle animations
- Use screen recordings with mouse pointer visible

VOICE-OVER TONE:
- Friendly and professional
- Enthusiastic but not overwhelming
- Clear pronunciation, moderate pace

LANGUAGES:
This script can be adapted for:
- Spanish (ES)
- French (FR)
- Catalan (CA)
- English (EN)
