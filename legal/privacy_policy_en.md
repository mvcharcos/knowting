# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 10, 2026_

Welcome to **Knowting**. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our Platform.

---

## 1. Introduction

Knowting ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies to all users of our web application for creating and taking educational tests.

By using the Platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When you sign in using Google authentication, we collect:
- **Email address**: Used as your unique identifier and username
- **Display name**: Your name as provided by Google (you can change this later)
- **Profile picture**: Your Google profile image (optional, you can update or remove it)

### 2.2 Content You Create

When you use the Platform, we store:
- **Tests**: Titles, descriptions, authors, and settings
- **Questions**: Question text, answer options, correct answers, explanations, and topic tags
- **Study materials**: Videos, PDFs, and images you upload to support your tests
- **Programs**: Collections of tests you create

### 2.3 Learning Activity Data

To track your progress and provide statistics, we collect:
- **Answer history**: Your responses to questions, whether they were correct, and timestamps
- **Test sessions**: Records of when you take tests, including scores and duration
- **Statistics**: Aggregated performance data per test and topic

### 2.4 Collaboration Data

When you share content with others:
- **Collaborator invitations**: Email addresses of users you invite
- **Roles and permissions**: The access level you grant to collaborators
- **Invitation status**: Whether invitations are pending or accepted

### 2.5 Preferences

- **Favorite tests**: Tests you mark as favorites
- **Language preference**: Your selected interface language

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- **Provide the service**: Enable you to create, take, and share tests
- **Track progress**: Show your learning statistics, scores, and performance over time
- **Enable collaboration**: Allow you to invite others to view or edit your content
- **Improve the Platform**: Understand how features are used to make improvements
- **Communicate with you**: Send important account-related notifications

We do **not** use your data for:
- Selling to third parties
- Advertising or marketing purposes
- Automated decision-making that affects you

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in a secure database. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

### 4.2 Data Retention

- **Account data**: Retained until you delete your account
- **Learning history**: Retained to show your progress; deleted when you delete your account
- **Content you create**: Retained until you delete it or your account

### 4.3 Uploaded Files

Study materials (videos, PDFs, images) are stored on our servers. When you delete a material or your account, these files are permanently removed.

---

## 5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

### 5.1 Google Authentication

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. When you log in, Google may collect data according to their own privacy policy. We only receive your email, name, and profile picture from Google.

### 5.2 Hosting Provider

Our Platform runs on cloud infrastructure. Your data may be processed by our hosting provider solely to provide the service.

### 5.3 No Sale of Data

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

### 5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

### 6.1 Access Your Data

You can view your profile information, learning history, and content through the Platform interface.

### 6.2 Update Your Data

You can update your display name and profile picture in your profile settings.

### 6.3 Delete Your Data

You can delete your account at any time from your profile settings. This will permanently remove:
- Your user account
- All tests and programs you created
- All questions and materials you uploaded
- Your answer history and statistics
- Your collaborator entries and favorites

### 6.4 Data Portability

If you need a copy of your data, contact us at the email below.

### 6.5 Withdraw Consent

You can stop using the Platform at any time. Deleting your account removes your data from our systems.

## 7. Cookies and Local Storage

The Platform uses browser session storage to maintain your login state and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for users aged **16 years or older**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to have it removed.
